Bays for a better Ocean

Cleaner bays are areas of the ocean that are designated for the purpose of removing debris and pollutants from the water. They can be used for a variety of activities, including cleaning up oil spills, removing plastic waste, and cleaning up hazardous materials.

Oil Spill Recovery Bays:

Designed specifically to clean up oil spills and other hydrocarbon-based pollutants.

Explore Now!

Floating Treatment Bays:

These bays consist of floating platforms equipped with filtration systems that remove pollutants from the water.

Explore Now!

Living Shoreline Bays:

These bays use natural elements such as oyster reefs, mangroves, and marsh grasses to filter pollutants and protect coastal areas.

Explore Now!

Bioremediation Bays:

These bays use microorganisms to break down pollutants in the water.
